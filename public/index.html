    🐝 Welcome to the Spelling Bee App! 🎉

    The Spelling Bee App is a fun and interactive way to test your spelling skills!

    🎮 Game Modes

    • Solo Mode: Play alone and practice at your own pace.
    • Contest Mode: Play with 2–5 participants and compete for the top score!

    👥 Managing Participants

    • Add, edit, or remove players before starting a contest.
    • When done adding participants, Submit participants to start a contest.

    🔢 Picking Questions

    • Select a number from the grid — each number is a word to spell.

    ⏱️ Starting the Spell

    • Click "Start Spelling" to begin.
    • The timer starts counting down.
    • Click "Check Answer" or wait for the timer to auto-check your spelling.

    🏆 Scoring & Results

    • Points are awarded for correct answers.
    • Scores are tracked for all players.
    • Once all questions are done, or you click "End Contest", the app announces the winner!

    It’s simple, fun, and great for both learning and competing with friends! 💡✨
